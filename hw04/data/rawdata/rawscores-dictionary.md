## Data `rawscores.csv`

- `HW1` to `HW9`: homework assignments, each 100 pts
- `ATT`: lab attendance, number of attended labs (0 to 12)
- `QZ1` to `QZ4`: quiz scores - quiz 1: 12 pts
                                quiz 2: 18 pts
                                quiz 3: 20 pts
                                quiz 4: 20 pts
- `EX1`: exam 1, 80 pts
- `EX2`: exam 2, 90 pts
